## **Project Name**: _My Personal Introduction Webpage_

_My professor and peers do not have all the adequate information that is needed to truly understand what makes me, me. The main priority is to provide a better overall idea of the little details that define who I am as a student and human being and what my career aspirations and spare-time activities include._

_I will successfully communicate what makes me who I am by providing insight on things such as what major is, my personal course and career goals and by detailing what my specific hobbies and interests are._

* _Successfully convey my aspirations within my major and what my career goals are_
* _Provide insightful details about personal hobbies and interests_
