A Look Into My CMPA Journey

When I first began my journey into the CMPA world, I honestly was quite unsure of what to expect. Although I have always had a deep fascination and love of computers, I had never truly examined the benefits of entering into a technology-based career. Enrolling in the online CMPA program through Texas Tech University gave me the opportunity to further delve into this possibility.

After just three weeks into my course on the fundamentals of computing applications, I fully believe a career in software programming is in my future. Seeing how prominent AI is becoming in today's society makes me aspire to be one of the software professionals responsible for making sure our technology sources are functioning in the most efficient way possible. I am excited and hopeful about the possibility of someday working for a major company that will help to further advance my career.

My main hope for this course is to gain every ounce of knowledge that is available to me so that I am better able to make an impact on our future world. I hope to be able to implement the critical thinking aspect and hands-on training that the course offers to help me obtain my degree and land a job in the highly sought-after technological world.

Among the aspirations and career goals I have for my CMPA journey, there are a few key activities I enjoy participating in that help to better describe who I am. These activities include:

  • Working out and following a strict diet regimen has been something that has really grounded me and helped to keep me focused on the long-term life goals I have for myself. Lifting weights regularly and implementing HIIT cardio routines is something I really enjoy because it allows my body to release endorphins and keep me fit and feeling my best
  • Going to sporting events with my family and friends has always been a favorite past-time. I grew up in Lubbock, Texas regularly attending any and all Texas Tech sporting events. As a native Texan, I also enjoy traveling to watch Texas pro sports teams compete. I enjoy attending these events with my family because it allows for us to have fun in a competitive environment
  • Golfing is something I grew up playing with my dad and siblings. I enjoy playing golf because it is a challenging skill sport that requires consistency and practice and is a great way to relieve stress and work on your hand-eye coordination.
